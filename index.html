Privacy Policy for Vetamon

Effective Date: January 2025

Introduction

Vetamon ("we," "our," or "us") is a veterinary practice management software designed exclusively for use by authorized veterinary clinic employees. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

Scope of Use

Vetamon is a closed-system application available only to:

  • Authorized employees of registered veterinary clinics
  • Users with credentials provided by their veterinary practice

This application does not accept public registrations or consumer accounts.

Information We Collect

Account Information

User accounts are created and managed exclusively by veterinary clinic administrators. We store:

  • Employee username (provided by clinic)
  • Encrypted authentication credentials
  • Role and permission settings assigned by clinic administrators

Usage Data

To maintain system security and functionality, we automatically collect:

  • Login timestamps and session duration
  • App version and device type
  • Error logs and crash reports (without personal information)
  • Feature usage statistics for app improvement

Clinic Data

Users may access and input veterinary practice data including:

  • Patient (animal) records
  • Appointment schedules
  • Medical records and treatment notes
  • Inventory and billing information

This data belongs to the veterinary clinic and is governed by the agreement between the clinic and our service.

How We Use Information

We use collected information solely to:

  • Provide veterinary practice management services
  • Authenticate users and maintain security
  • Provide technical support to clinics
  • Monitor and improve app performance
  • Comply with legal obligations

Data Storage and Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Secure authentication protocols
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and permission management

All data is stored on secure servers with restricted access.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties except:

  • With the veterinary clinic that employs the user
  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect the rights and safety of our service and users
  • With service providers who assist in operating our application (under strict confidentiality agreements)

User Rights and Controls

As an employee user:

  • Your account is managed by your clinic administrator
  • Account modifications must be requested through your clinic
  • You can log out of the application at any time
  • You should follow your clinic's data handling policies

Data Retention

User account data is retained as long as:

  • The clinic maintains an active subscription
  • The employee remains authorized by the clinic

Upon termination of clinic subscription or employee access, data is retained according to legal requirements and then securely deleted.

Children's Privacy

Vetamon is a professional application for veterinary employees aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. This application is not intended for use by minors.

International Data Transfers

If you access Vetamon from outside our primary operating country, please note that your information may be transferred to and processed in countries with different data protection laws.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify clinic administrators of significant changes, who should inform their employees. Continued use of the application after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Compliance

This privacy policy is designed to comply with applicable data protection laws including GDPR (where applicable) and relevant veterinary data protection standards.

Acknowledgment

By using Vetamon, you acknowledge that you are an authorized employee of a registered veterinary clinic and agree to handle all data in accordance with your clinic's policies and applicable laws.
